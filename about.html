About Beck Lewis

Crime fiction about ordinary people facing extraordinary choices.

Hello

I’m Beck Lewis, author of the Dead Ends series and the Female Rage thrillers.

I write crime fiction about ordinary people caught in extraordinary circumstances — stories where the mystery matters, but the people matter even more.

Whether I’m writing a police investigation, a revenge thriller or something in between, I want every book to leave readers feeling they’ve been taken on a satisfying journey by someone they can trust.

Why I write

Writing has been part of my life for most of my adult years.

For me, stories are a way of understanding the world. They let us examine injustice, explore difficult questions and experience lives that aren’t our own.

I believe readers deserve stories that respect both their intelligence and their time. A Beck Lewis novel should be gripping from the opening chapter, emotionally rewarding by the final page, and always feel like it’s in safe hands.

A Midlands writer

I’m proud to write stories rooted in the Midlands.

The places, communities and people here shape my work. Rather than treating the Midlands as somewhere characters pass through, I like making it central to the story.

The books

The Dead Ends series follows three detectives who have all, in different ways, been pushed to the margins.

DS Rob Joseph

Rob has reinvented himself so many times he’s no longer sure who he really is.

DC Josie Kaminski

Josie is brilliant, fearless and permanently one sentence away from offending someone important.

DC Walker Smith

Walker is approaching retirement while carrying mistakes from his past that refuse to stay buried.

Together they investigate the cases nobody else wants while navigating lives that are every bit as complicated as the crimes they solve.

Alongside Dead Ends, the Female Rage books explore injustice from a very different perspective — darkly funny revenge thrillers about women who decide they’ve had enough.

Beyond the page

Before becoming a full-time writer I worked in a variety of jobs, each one teaching me something about people — their fears, ambitions, habits and secrets.

Today you’ll often find me making short films, filming social media content, speaking at festivals and libraries, chatting with readers, or dreaming up unusual ways to bring stories to life.

I’ve previously written bestselling fiction under other names, some of which has been optioned for television. Beck Lewis is where my fascination with crime, justice and human nature comes together.
