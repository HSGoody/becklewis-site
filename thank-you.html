Almost there.

To get Man or Bear, you need to confirm your email address.

1

Open your inbox and look for an email from AWeber / Beck Lewis.

It usually arrives within a minute or two.

2

Click the confirmation link inside that email.

That is the step that unlocks the download.

3

Then check your inbox again for the download email.

If you do not see it, give it another minute.

Cannot find the confirmation email?

  • Check your spam/junk folder.
  • Check Promotions (Gmail) or Other/Clutter (some mail apps).
  • Search your inbox for aweber or beck lewis.

Already confirmed?

If you have confirmed and still have not received the download email, wait 2 minutes and check spam/junk again.

You can unsubscribe at any time.
