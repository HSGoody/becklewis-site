To get Man or Bear, confirm your email address.

Step 1: Find the confirmation email

Look for an email from AWeber / Beck Lewis. It usually arrives within a minute or two.

Step 2: Click confirm

Click the confirmation link inside that email. This is the step that unlocks the download.

Step 3: Get the download email

After confirming, check your inbox again for the email containing the download link.

Still cannot find it?

Check spam/junk, Promotions/Other, and search for aweber or beck lewis.
