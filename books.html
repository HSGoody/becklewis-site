Double Take

Dead Ends 1

A psychological thriller by Beck Lewis

When what you saw was impossible, is honesty always the best option?

When Esther Butler sees a man with a gun following a woman in the dark city streets, she doesn’t hesitate to call the police.

Minutes later in an empty building site, the man is dead. The police fired the shot. But when the smoke clears, there’s no weapon to be found.

Esther knows what she saw. Yet as her story begins to unravel, she’s forced to question not just her memory, but her own safety.

DC Josie Kaminski – assigned to the police team with the worst reputation in Leicester – is put on the case.

As truth and lies blur, the team must decide who to believe.

Open Wound

Dead Ends 2

A psychological thriller by Beck Lewis

Can saving someone be the biggest mistake of your life?

PR consultant Hannah Douglas-Jones stops at the scene of a house fire and pulls a young woman from the flames.

Natasha – calm, capable and self-assured – is now homeless. Hannah offers her a place to stay and a job.

As Hannah’s fortunes rise, she starts to doubt who this woman really is – and what she wants.

Six people died in the fire. It was no accident.

Safe Space

Dead Ends 3

A psychological thriller by Beck Lewis

When your memories are missing, how do you know you’re not the one to blame?

A year after a carbon monoxide accident, Cassie O’Donnell wakes from a coma to find her life erased.

She joins a survivors’ support group – a safe space where secrets fester.

When one member dies, suspicion turns inward.

No one is safe.
