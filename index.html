Beck Lewis - Psychological Thriller Author

I'm Beck Lewis, a British psychological thriller author who has spent most of my adult life immersed in stories. Writing is the way I make sense of the world. I believe deeply that stories exist to connect people, to help us see the world through perspectives that are not our own.

I've spent years in writing groups, workshops, conventions, and creative spaces, learning both from established voices and from those taking their first steps. Readers are at the heart of everything I do.

Creativity doesn't start or stop with writing for me. It spills into short films, social media videos, and collaborative projects. If you see me at an event, there's a good chance something fun or unusual is happening.

Writing is now my full-time work. I've written many books-some bestselling, some optioned-and I care deeply about telling stories that matter.
